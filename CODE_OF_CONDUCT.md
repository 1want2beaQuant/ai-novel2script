# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, practical collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat other contributors with courtesy.
- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence and specific examples.
- Keep reviews focused on code, behavior, tests, documentation, and project goals.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive information without permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private or security-sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove inappropriate content, limit participation, or block contributors when needed to protect the project and its users. Reports should include the relevant links, screenshots, or context needed to review the issue.
